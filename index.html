EXPERIENCE

Software Development

I am currently working as a programmer at Ontario Shores Center for Mental Health Sciences. I have used PowerShell and Summit Scripting Toolkit to automate staffing processes while also contributing to some website design projects using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.

In the past I have used Python and Java to complete school and personal projects. I am in the process of designing a dating app with a friend that requires the use of both Java and React.

Management

My first job came in grade 10 at a Victoria McDonald's. By the time I was in grade 12 I was one of the key managers for my branch, handling multiple programs and closing the restaurant nightly. Before leaving the job to travel for a year, I was offered the position of head manager.

In the eight months preceding university I worked two jobs simultaneously. One job involved marketing for a start-up house maintenance business. Within a month of being hired to as a marketer for this company I was asked to manage the marketing team.

Music

Throughout my time in high school I was involved in the Victoria music scene. I was a member of the Vic High R & B band for five years as the lead guitarist. During my time in the band, we travelled to Ontario, Tennessee, Alabama, Arkansas, and Mississippi to record and perform.

I was also the performing bass player and studio guitar player for Close the Bombay Doors, a band composed of young musicians from Victoria, BC. We released an EP in 2016 and performed across Vancouver Island and in Seattle, Washington.

Travel

Between high school and university I took a year off to work and travel. Over the course of 6 months abroad, I visited 22 countries and made some incredible memories while visiting places like Venice, Chernobyl, Yangshuo, and Pyongyang. With each new adventure my friends and I liked to strive out of our comfort zones a little bit more and explore the unknown. I developed an interest in the applications of communism and can say I have been to Vietnam, China, North Korea, and Cuba which are all either former or current communist states.

University

I was accepted into the program after achieving excellent grades in 6 core first-year courses ranging from calculus to linear algebra. I also secured an eight month co-op job at Ontario Shores Centre for Mental Health Sciences. After my work term concludes, I plan to return to UTSC to continue in computer science.
