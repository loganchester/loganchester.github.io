ABOUT ME

Welcome to my tiny corner of the web!

I grew up in Victoria, BC, Canada. During high school, I mixed my passion for academics with music while I spent four years performing in the Victoria High School Rhythm and Blues Band. Additionally, I formed many small bands and even released an EP.

Following high school, I took a year off to travel. During this time, I visited to over 20 countries! Have a look through my travel blogs.

My next adventure required me to move across Canada to attend school at the Univeristy of Toronto and persue a degree in Computer Science, specializing in Software Engineering. I am currently in the fourth-year of my degree.
