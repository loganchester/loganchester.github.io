ABOUT ME



Welcome to my tiny corner of the web! This site is a simple representation of me and some of my accomplishments...

I grew up in Victoria, BC, Canada. During high school, I mixed my passion for academics with music while I spent four years performing in the Victoria High School Rhythm and Blues Band. Additionally, I formed many small bands and even released an EP on Spotify and Apple Music with "Close the Bombay Doors".

Following high school, I took a year off to travel and enjoy a much needed rest from school. During this time, I travelled to over 20 countries! You can find pictures of these travels in the blog section...

My next adventure required me to move across Canada to attend school at the Univeristy of Toronto and persue a degree in computer science, specializing in software engineering.

Although I am still a student in Toronto, I am currently taking a semester abroad to study at the University of Otago in Dunedin, New Zealand! Check out the travel blog for pictures of my latest endevour!
