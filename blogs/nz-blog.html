NEW ZEALAND

These photos offer just a small sample of my experiences in New Zealand! Included in the images are Lake Pukaki, Mt. Cook/Aoraki, Mt. Sefton, Mt. Aspiring National Park, Moeraki, Lake Hawea, Lake Tekapo, and Dunedin.



I wanted to include a small section dedicated to someone very special. She made saying goodbye to Canada so hard despite the excitement of beginning a new adventure in New Zealand. Adele was the last person I said goodbye to in Victoria and I hope that she is the first person to greet me when I get back!
