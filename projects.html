This Website

This website is one of the first projects I worked on. I created it to showcase my programming skills to prospective employers. Over three years, it has undergone a number of changes. You can take a look at the source code by visiting the github link at the bottom of this section.

I began the process of building a website in my first-year of university. At the time, I had no experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, or UI/UX. I started from scratch and learned through online tutorials and plenty of trial and error. Over time I have gained skills in each of these concepts. I would say that my foremost interest is web development and UI/UX, and that probably has something to do with my experience building this site.

Although this site did not help me achieve my first internship at Ontario Shores, it did help me to secure my second internship at Kontrol Energy. As a Full-Stack Developer I spend a significant amount of time on both design and front-end implementation.

Various - Ontario Shores Centre for Mental Health Sciences

As a Business Analyst and Developer at Ontario Shores, I wore many hats and contributed to many different projects. My main role was to build a system of scripts to automate staffing processes using PowerShell and Summit Scripting Toolkit. By implementing this system, I saved the IT department multiple hours daily and freed up another analyst to take care of other critical staffing tasks.

Additionally, I contributed to ontarioshores.ca by enhancing useability and cleaning up the codebase. I was also a valuable member of the IT service team and developed crucial customer service skills and learned to troubleshoot hardware and software problems with ease.

Planit

In my third year at university, I was introduced to software development while building a mobile app called "Planit". This app was built using React Native and Java. The development team consisted of 7 students, including myself. We followed agile best-practices and presented our product to the client at the end of the semester.

This experience highlighted the challenges of agile development and helped prepare me for my Co-op experience as a Full-Stack Developer at Kontrol Energy. As the scrum master for this particular project, I was responsible for maintaining order within our development team and ensuring that best practices were followed, while learning React Native on the fly. I believe that this was a valuable experience in my journey towards a career in software development.
